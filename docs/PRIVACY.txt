🔒 API DEBUGGER - PRIVACY POLICY 🔒

Last updated: March 2024

═══════════════════════════════════════════

👉 WHAT WE DON'T DO (Important)

❌ We don't see your data
   This extension runs entirely in your browser.
   Your API calls never touch our servers.

❌ We don't collect your requests
   All data stays on YOUR device.

❌ We don't track you
   No analytics. No telemetry. No crashes reports.
   Nothing.

❌ We don't see your API keys
   If you add an AI key, it goes DIRECTLY to the
   AI provider. We never touch it.

❌ We don't know what APIs you debug
   We have ZERO visibility into your usage.

═══════════════════════════════════════════

📦 DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored ONLY in your browser:

• Collections, requests, environments → chrome.storage.sync
  (Your browser, YOUR Google account if synced)

• Request history → chrome.storage.local
  (Stays on your device, never leaves)

• Settings → chrome.storage.sync

═══════════════════════════════════════════

🤖 AI FEATURES

If you use AI:

• You bring YOUR OWN API key (e.g., OpenRouter)
• Requests go DIRECTLY from your browser to AI provider
• We have ZERO access to your key or AI conversations
• Your AI provider's privacy policy applies

═══════════════════════════════════════════

🔗 GITHUB SYNC

If you enable GitHub sync:

• YOU provide your GitHub personal access token
• Token stays in YOUR browser
• We NEVER see your token
• Data goes to YOUR GitHub repository
